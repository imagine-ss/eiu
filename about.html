About University
We are a research institution focused on providing massive opportunities through value education. Equatoria International University is one of South Sudan’s preeminent private universities.
Our impact on individuals, our region and the world is profound — whether we are launching young people into a boundless future or confronting the grand challenges of our time through undaunted research and innovation. We turn ideas into impact and transform lives and our nation.
In a sense, Equatoria International University's greatest invention may be itself — an unusual concentration of unusual talent, forever reinventing itself on a mission to make a better world. That invention is powered by individuals: a global community of educators, researchers, and learners with different perspectives but a shared commitment to serving humanity. Come, join us on the journey.

VISION

To be a centre of professional and academic excellence.

MISSION

To provide and advance knowledge, skills and research in humanities and social sciences and technology for the improvement and development of society.

OUR MOTTO

Education for enhancing the future.

AIM OF THE UNIVERSITY

The university's aim is to cause an impact in the lives of people in the society through education by providing higher education services maintenances knowledge and skills and also instills confidence humility respect obedience, dedication, courage and commitment in serving society and the world at large.
